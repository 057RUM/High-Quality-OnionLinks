Oh and like I said before onion links in a more advanced definition are basically websites on the dark web that use the “.onion” top-level domain instead of “.com,” “.net,” “.gov,” etc. So as a result Onion sites use The Onion Router (Tor) software to encrypt their connections and enable anonymous communication. They also hide their location, ownership, and other identifiers. By the way Onion routing is a sophisticated means to prevent tracking your location, but there's no such thing as perfect online anonymity. Although your internet traffic is encrypted on Tor, your ISP can still see that you're connected to Tor so be careful
